Egg Sandwich

This yummy egg sandwich is perfect to kick off the mornings. With bacon and egg it has plenty of protein and would pair perfectly with a side of fruit.

Anyone can make this simple recipe. All that is required is a pan, a toaster can be used as well to speed things up.

Ingredients:

  • Bagel
  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Franks Red Hot (or your prefered hot sauce)
  • Avocado
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Melt butter on a pan on medium heat and crack and egg inside. Add salt and pepper to egg.
  2. Start cooking bacon in a seperate pan.
  3. Put bagel in toaster or manually toast on pan if you don't have a toaster available.
  4. Flip egg once it starts to bubble and cook for 2 minutes on the other side.
  5. Remove cooked bacon from pan and wrap with paper towels to remove grease.
  6. Compile bagel with egg, cheese, bacon, avocado and dabble Franks Red Hot on top.

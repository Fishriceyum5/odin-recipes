Grandma's Guac Recipe

This simple guac recipie works percect as a quick party snack or to add flavor to your mexican night.

The jalapenos and cilantro are optional to this recipie if they are not one of your favorites!

Ingredients:

  • Avocado
  • lime
  • salt
  • pepper
  • tomato
  • onion
  • cilantro (optional)
  • Jalapeno (optional)

Steps:

  1. Smash up avocados
  2. Dice up tomato and onion and add to smashed avocados
  3. Squeeze lime and add salt and pepper and stir up guac
  4. Optional: add in jalapeno and/or cilantro

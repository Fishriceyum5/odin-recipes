Declicous and Easy Margarita Pizza

This margarita pizza can be made in a standard oven with everyday igredients. Homemade pizza's have their challenges, but this one should be easy to follow with the step by step recipe.

It is recomended to have baking steels for this recipe. They can tend to be pretty expensive, however it is well worth the investment.

Pizza Ingredients:

  • all-purpose flour
  • granulated sugar
  • acitve dry yeast
  • kosher salt
  • extra virgin olive oil

Sauce Ingredients:

  • pureed or crushed canned San Marzano tomatoes
  • fresh garlic cloves
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • kisher salt
  • ground black pepper

Toppings Ingredients:

  • finely grated parmigiano-reggiano cheese
  • fresh mozzarella cheese
  • large fresh basil leaves
  • dried red pepper flakes

Steps:

  1. Prepare Pizza Dough
  2. Preheat Oven and Pizza Steel or Stone
  3. screape the dough onto a well-floured counter top and knead the dough for three minutes.
  4. lgihtyly grease a large mixing bowl with olive oil, and place the dough into the bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and allow the dough to rise and warm.
  5. assemble the pizza and add toppings
